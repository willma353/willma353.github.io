Research

I work on the theory of online algorithms and decision-making under uncertainty. I develop data-driven methods for problems in operations and revenue management, including inventory, e-commerce fulfillment, assortment optimization, and online resource allocation. I have partnered with businesses including Alibaba, Bed Bath & Beyond, and Dream11 to deploy algorithms from my research. I am interested in improving business decisions through LLMs and AI, and part of the research team at percepta.ai.
